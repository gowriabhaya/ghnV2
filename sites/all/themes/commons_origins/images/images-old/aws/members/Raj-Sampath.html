Chief Technology Officer
Raj Sampath is a technology professional, technology innovator and social entrepreneur. Currently he is focused on social innovation using technology.

As a director at TechSoup Global (TSG) he spearheads technology development that support TSG mission of empowering NPO/NGO with innovative technologies worldwide. His belief is that modern information technology disinter mediates the redundant layers and brings producers and consumers close to each other. This concept is applicable to all segments globally. Entrepreneurs, who understand this paradigm change, stand to benefit immensely by creating value using this technology.

His engagement in Global Heart Network (GHN) is born out of that belief. GHN mission is to create an efficient ecosystem by leveraging this evolving technology among highly specialized healthcare providers, funders, enablers and needy patients in under privileged circumstance. In the past Raj led technology innovations in consumer finance segment in start-up and large company environments. He co-founded nCommand the Electronic Partner Network (EPN), which set the direction for the modern technology standards for the loan origination process.

Raj has worked in four countries and has developed Global perspective by frequent travels to different countries. So far he has travelled to 20 countries. He serves as a voluntary board member at Fort Mason Center. He lends his thoughts and oversees Marketing & Development sub-committee.

He lives with his wife and son, enjoys watching his son play American Football and Soccer, taking his dog for long walks and playing golf with his friends. Raj holds a BS in Statistics from Madras Christian College (India), a Masters in Computer Applications from Birla Institute of Technology (India), and an MBA from the Marriott School of Management at Brigham Young University.
