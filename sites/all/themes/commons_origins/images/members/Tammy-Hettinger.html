Development Advisor
Tammy Hettinger has more than 10 years of success in fundraising for nonprofit organizations including the Campaign for Tobacco-Free Kids, Children’s HeartLink and the National Democratic Institute for International Affairs. She also serves on the planning committee for the TEDx Adams Morgan event Forces of Change to be held in June 2012 in Washington, DC.

Tammy became passionate about helping children with heart disease and increasing access to cardiac care in underserved areas of the world during her tenure at Children’s HeartLink. In collaboration with other members of the Global Heart Network’s Management Team, she will work to raise awareness and funds to support GHN’s mission.

Tammy earned a Master’s Degree in Public Policy at the University of London and a BA in Political Science at the University of Minnesota. She currently lives in Arlington, VA with her husband and daughter.
